Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Worthia (“Worthia”, “we”, “us”) is a read-only cryptocurrency portfolio tracker. You add public wallet addresses and Worthia shows their balances and value. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

We never ask for, receive, or store your private keys, seed phrases, or recovery phrases. Worthia cannot move, spend, or access your funds.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you create an account we store your email address and display name. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique account identifier and, where you permit it, your name and email.
  • Wallet addresses. The public blockchain addresses you choose to add. These are public information. We use them to look up balances, tokens, and NFTs.
  • Portfolio & preferences. Balances, tokens, NFTs, and portfolio-value history derived from your addresses, plus app settings such as currency, theme, and language. This is synced to your account so it is available across your devices.
  • Push notification token. If you enable notifications, we store a device push token so we can send you portfolio price alerts.
  • Subscription status. Whether you have an active Worthia Pro subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple; we do not receive your payment card or billing details.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core service — fetching balances and displaying your portfolio.
  • To sync your data securely across your devices.
  • To send price-movement alerts when you enable notifications.
  • To manage Worthia Pro subscriptions and entitlements.
  • To respond to support requests.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

Third-party services

Worthia relies on the following processors to operate. Your wallet addresses are sent to blockchain and price providers solely to fetch balances and prices:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions) — account sign-in and secure storage of your synced portfolio. Data is processed in the europe-west1 region.
  • Alchemy, DeBank, CoinGecko, blockchain.info — blockchain balance, token, NFT, and price data.
  • RevenueCat and Apple — subscription management and billing.
  • Apple and Google — optional Sign in with Apple / Google.
  • Expo push service — delivery of notifications to your device.

Data retention and your choices

  • Notifications. Turn price alerts on or off at any time in Settings.
  • Delete your account. Settings → Delete Account permanently deletes your account and all synced data (wallet addresses, portfolio history, and preferences) from our systems. This cannot be undone.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit. Access to your synced data is restricted to your authenticated account. Worthia also offers an optional Face ID / biometric app lock.

Children

Worthia is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
